Persepolis

Persepolis, a UNESCO World Heritage site, represents the grandeur of the Achaemenid Empire. Located near Shiraz, it features magnificent palaces and columns.

Entry Fee: $5

Naqsh-e Jahan Square

This iconic square in Isfahan is a masterpiece of Persian architecture, surrounded by stunning mosques and historical bazaars.

Entry Fee: $3
